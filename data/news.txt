Headline: Kweichow Moutai Faces Supply Chain Disruption
Date: April 20, 2026

Summary:
Kweichow Moutai (600519.SH) is expected to face temporary headwinds in Q2 2026. 
According to industry insiders, a logistical bottleneck in the Guizhou province 
has delayed shipments of the flagship 53% ABV liquor by approximately two weeks. 
Analysts at Golden Sun Securities have downgraded the stock from "Strong Buy" to "Hold" 
citing potential revenue misses for the quarter. However, long-term demand remains robust.
